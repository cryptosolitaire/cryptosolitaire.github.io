What is CryptoSolitaire

CryptoSolitaire combines art and Blockchain technology to provide players with a unique experience. You'll be able to play our card games using Non-Fungible Tokens designed by talented artists. Collaborating with these artists will give the decks an intrinsic value. And as they become more popular in their craft, the value of the decks they created will also rise alongside it.

  • Own

    By applying Blockchain technology, you'll be able to truly own the playing cards. Only you have control over the decks you collect. Nobody can delete the cards, not even us.

  • Play

    Unlike Bitcoin and other fungible tokens which can only represent value, our decks have real utility. You can play with them in our card games and they are beautiful to look at!

  • Earn

    Don't have time to play anymore? Then sell the decks you own on decentralized exchanges. You can recuperate your investment or earn a nice profit.

  • Support

    If you are a fan of the artist we feature, buying our decks will support their work. They are receiving a percentage of every sale guaranteed by smart contracts.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Non-Fungible Token?

Non-fungible means not interchangeable. They are unique items that live on the Blockchain. This is the opposite of Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies which can be replaced with an identical token.

How can I have true ownership of the cards?

The cards are Non-Fungible Tokens that are saved on the Ethereum Blockchain. Blockchain is a distributed and decentralized database technology that no one entity can control. This means only the owner can perform transactions with the cards.

What gives value to the cards?

True scarcity and the reputation of the artist who created the deck. Demand for the cards may also increase as the popularity of the artist increases.

What happens if CryptoSolitaire goes out of business?

You still own the cards. Blockchain technology ensures you have full control over your digital assets even if our business stops operating. Other developers can also create games that can work with your NFT playing cards.
